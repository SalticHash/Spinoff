> A Jame for the Jame Gam

You play as a hamster called Rick, after you start running in your hamster wheel it ends up breaking!

Spin around a parkour course and reach the end!

Instructions:
Wait for the dialog to end, press W to spin forward.

* W, S move in direction
* A, D turn in direction
* Space jump

(Due to complications the game is very half baked but it is a nice short MVP)
